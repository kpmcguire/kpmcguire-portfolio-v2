Find Causes You Love!

GiveGab recommends volunteer opportunities based on your skills and interests, better connecting you with Nonprofits whose mission you care about.

Invite Others

Send invites to all of your friends to join GiveGab, because volunteering is more fun with friends! Build a larger community of volunteers to make a bigger impact on the world around you.

 

Track Your Impact

Track your impact to motivate yourself, inspire your friends, and help promote the causes you care about most.

Reminders

Receive email reminders for your upcoming volunteer opportunities and events, logging hours, and other important activities. We'll make sure you never forget to volunteer!

Fun and Games

Compete with your friends and other volunteers on leaderboards. Collect rewards and build a trophy collection by volunteering and logging your hours on GiveGab.

Show Your Story

If a picture is worth a thousand words, what's a video worth? Take pride in your achievements through photos & videos to inspire others.

Build a Volunteer Profile

Keep a reflections journal of your volunteer experiences and create a resume for employment and graduate school applications. A recent study by the CNCS shows volunteers have a 27 percent higher likelihood of finding a job after being out of work than non-volunteers!

Social Sharing

Share exciting volunteer events and opportunities through GiveGab and other social networks like Twitter and Facebook. Let the world know what's happening at the nonprofits you care about and let your friends know where they can volunteer with you!
